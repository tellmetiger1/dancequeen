Frequently Asked Questions

What should my child wear to class?
Students should wear comfortable dance attire such as leotards, tights, and ballet shoes for ballet classes, or comfortable athletic wear and jazz shoes for other styles. Hair should be pulled back securely.
Do you offer trial classes?
Yes! We offer trial classes for new students. Contact us to schedule a trial class and see if Dance Queen Q is the right fit for you.
What is your class size policy?
We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Most classes have 8-12 students maximum, allowing for individual feedback and instruction.
How do I know which level class to register for?
Our instructors will evaluate your child's skill level during the first class and recommend the appropriate level. We can always adjust if needed to ensure the best learning experience.
What is your payment and refund policy?
Monthly tuition is due at the beginning of each month. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new students. Please contact us for specific payment terms and policies.
Do you have recitals or performances?
Yes! We hold annual recitals and participate in various community performances throughout the year. Students also have opportunities to compete in regional and national competitions.
