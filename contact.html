Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a trial class?
Contact us through the form above or call us directly to schedule a trial class. We'll help you choose the right class based on your age and experience level.
What should I bring to my first class?
For your first class, wear comfortable clothes you can move in. We'll provide guidance on proper dance attire once you decide to join our classes.
Do you offer classes for adults?
Yes! We offer adult classes in Jazz, Ballet, Hip Hop, and Contemporary. These classes are designed for adults of all skill levels.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24-hour notice for class cancellations. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule.
Do you have parking available?
Yes, we have convenient parking available in front of our studio. The parking lot is well-lit and easily accessible.
How do I know which class level is right for me?
Our instructors will evaluate your skill level during your first class and recommend the appropriate level. We can always adjust if needed.
